Strawberry Custard

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp custard powder

Instructions:

  1. Blend strawberries into a puree.
  2. Boil milk and add custard powder mixed with a little cold milk.
  3. Add sugar and cook till thickened.
  4. Mix in strawberry puree and let it cool.
  5. Refrigerate and serve chilled.
