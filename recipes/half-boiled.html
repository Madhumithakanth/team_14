Half Boiled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Water for boiling
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Gently place the eggs into the boiling water.
  3. Cook for 4-5 minutes for a soft, healthy-salad texture.
  4. Remove the eggs and place them in cold water to stop the cooking process.
  5. Crack the shell, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and enjoy.
